Worko: Reporting Platform for Students & Teachers

Worko is an innovative platform designed to facilitate seamless communication between students and teachers. Students can submit reports, track their progress, and communicate directly with teachers.

Worko helps students and teachers report issues such as gaming addiction, bullying, and other concerns. With real-time notifications, both students and teachers stay connected for efficient communication.

How to Use Worko

1. Visit the Site

Go to the homepage of Worko. You'll be greeted with options to sign up or sign in. https://mlnitoon2.github.io

2. Sign Up (For New Users)

If you are new to Worko, click the "Sign Up" button. You will need to provide the following information:

  • Name
  • Email
  • Password
Once you submit the form, you will be registered as a user, and you can sign in.

3. Sign In (For Returning Users)

If you already have an account, click the "Sign In" button. Enter your registered email and password to log in.

4. Student Dashboard

Once logged in as a student, you'll be able to submit reports, track their status, and manage your class codes.

5. Teacher Dashboard

Teachers can view and respond to reports from students, generate class codes, and manage the platform's communication tools.

6. Managing Class Codes

Teachers can generate class codes for their classes. Students can add class codes to their settings and manage their classes as needed.
